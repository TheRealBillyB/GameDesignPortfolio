About Me

I'm a game designer passionate about creating deep, player-driven experiences. My work spans multiple genres, from voxel sandboxes to large-scale multiplayer shooters, always focusing on meaningful systems design and player agency.

What drives me is understanding the psychology behind game design. Why do players love grinding in one game but hate it in another? How do you balance challenge without frustration? I break down these questions through structured analysis and hands-on design work.

I'm particularly interested in teaching AI to understand game design. Games aren't just code and mechanics; they're human experiences wrapped in systems. Translating that intuition into structured reasoning is a challenge I'm excited to tackle.
